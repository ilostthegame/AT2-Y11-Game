Interpretation of file:
Double exclamation mark (!!) marks the ID of portal, e.g. !!00
ID is separated from portal object destination by tilde (~)
Template for portal object as below:
!!{ID}~{image_name}/{destination}/{requirement}
'requirement' is the required quest item to enter portal. May be 'None'
