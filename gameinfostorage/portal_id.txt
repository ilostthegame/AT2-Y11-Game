Interpretation of file:
Double exclamation mark (!!) marks the ID of portal, e.g. !!00
ID is separated from portal object destination by tilde (~)
Template for npc object as below:
!!{ID}~{image_name}/{destination}

!!D1~portal_2/Music Centre 1
!!D2~portal_2/some other world
!!M1~portal_2/Music Centre 2
!!M2~portal_2/Music Centre 3
!!M3~portal_2/Music Centre 4
