Interpretation of file:
Double exclamation mark (!!) marks the ID of quest item, e.g. !!00
ID is separated from quest item object information by tilde (~)
Quest item object information is found on same line, separated by forward slashes (/)
Template for quest item object as below:
!!{ID}~{image_name}/{name}

!!SP~stone_of_puzzling_longevity/Stone of Puzzling Longevity
!!GP~gem_of_persistent_tranquility/Gem of Persistent Tranquility
!!JV~jewel_of_vibrant_colours/Jewel of Vibrant Colours
!!CE~crystal_of_eternal_light/Crystal Of Eternal Light
